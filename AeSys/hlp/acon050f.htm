Draw Mode

You must be in Draw Mode to create new drawing elements. Select Mode from Menu Bar and click on Draw or Press the Home key or the [ key to enter Draw Mode. Your Mode is displayed on the Tool bar at the bottom of the screen.

The commands are listed as buttons on the Toolbar at the bottom of the screen.

You draw.things by moving the cursor and pressing keys. Move the cursor using the Mouse or the Arrow Keys. Press the keys on the 10 key keypad, the typewriter keyboard, or a 10 key Cursor Puck (if you have one).

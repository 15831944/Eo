Graphic Tab Function:

Press a key to move the cursor:

(plan ) North (top of sheet)

nw | ne

W E R

West--S D--East

Z X C

sw | se F Lower

South V Higher

Press . (period) to set the tab distance: (DimLen)

The initial value of DimLen is 1'0" .

Press > (greater) to set the tab angle: (DimAngZ)

The initial value of DimAngZ is 45 degrees.

Directions are valid in any view, even perspective, and can be used to compose in any view.

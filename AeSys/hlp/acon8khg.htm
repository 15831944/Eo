Pick and Drag a Primitive

Move cursor to any point on the primitive.

Press ; (the SEMICOLON key)

The primitive becomes a mobile.

The Toolbar displays commands

Move the cursor

The primitive is dragged with the cursor.

Press ENTER to complete

Press ESC to ABORT.

The primitive can be rotated, copied, flipped enlarged, and reduced on the fly .

One primitive in a segment can be edited without expanding the segment.

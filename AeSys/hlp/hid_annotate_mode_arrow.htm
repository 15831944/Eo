Annotate 3 Leader with Arrowhead

Move cursor to start of leader and press 3 .

Rubber banding is enabled

Move the cursor to the end of the leader and press 3 .

The leader is displayed between the two points 2 and 3 can be mixed to produce plain or arrow terminations on either end:

[2] -------> [3]

[3] <------- [2]

[3] <-----> [3]

Enter breaks the input.

Size and shape of the arrow are set using Annotate 0.

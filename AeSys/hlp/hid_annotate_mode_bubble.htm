Annotate 4 Draw an annotation Bubble

Press 4 to draw a BUBBLE at the cursor.

You are prompted for 2 character input.

Enter the characters to be in the bubble

The bubble is drawn in Pen 1.

The characters are posted in the BUBBLE in Pen 2.

The size and shape of the bubble are set using Annotate 0.

Note that regular polygons of any size and shape can be drawn.

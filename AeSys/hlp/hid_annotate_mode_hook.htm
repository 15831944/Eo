Annotate 5 Leader with Hook

Move cursor to start of leader and press 2 .

Rubber banding is enabled

Move the cursor to the end of the leader and press 5 .

The leader is displayed between the two points.

[2] and [5] can be mixed to produce:

[5] O--------O [5]

[2] ---------O [5]

[5] O--------- [2]

Size of the hook is set using Annotate 0.

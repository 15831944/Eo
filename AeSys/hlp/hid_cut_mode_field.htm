Cut 4 Cuts all segments at fence and traps those inside.

Move the cursor to one corner of the proposed field

Press 4.

Rubber banding is enabled.

Move the cursor to the diagonal corner of the field.

The rectangle defines the cut area.

Press 4 again to execute the cut.

All segments passing thru the fence are cut and added to the buffer.

Note that only rectangular fields normal to the major axes can be created. This function is not available for macro elements such as curves and text.

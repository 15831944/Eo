DIM 9 Converts a primitive into a dimension

Move the cursor to the TARGET line and press 9 .

The TARGET line is converted into a dimension line.

Press 9 again to convert the dimension back into a primitive.

You can do some rapid measurements by posting the dimension right on the segment. You can also use ; pick and copy, drag the copy to the dimension line location, convert it to a dimension line and put the arrows on it.

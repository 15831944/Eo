2 Draw2 Draw Parallel Lines

Move the cursor to start point of line and press 2 .

Rubber band is enabled.

Move the cursor to the end point of the line and press 2 .

Line is fixed and added to the workfile.

To continue drawing lines end-to-end:

Move the cursor to the next endpoint and press 2 .

Line is fixed and added to workfile.

Process continues until another Option is selected.

Press Enter to terminate line definitions.

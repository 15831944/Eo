Fixup 4-4 Fillet to intersect

Move the cursor to line and press 4.

The toolbar number is highlighted.

Move the cursor to other line and press 4

Both lines are cut back from the intersection and connected by a new fillet arc.

Use 1 4 instead of 4 4 to trim only the second line.

Use 2 4 to trim the first, fillet the second.

Use 4 2 to fillet the first, trim the second.

The radius of the arc is set using the Fixup 0 key.

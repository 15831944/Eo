Annotate Mode To decorate a drawing

Choose Annotate from MenuBar - Mode or

Press | or End from any GIN mode.

Press numeric keys to execute commands:

0 OptionsSet Annotate Mode options

1Tag Insert a Tag Marker

2-2 LeaderStart or end a plain leader.

2-3ArrowStart or end a leader with arrowhead.

4BubbleInsert a bubble into the workfile (prompted).

2-5HookStart or end a leader with a hook.

6UndlnUnderline text at the cursor position.

7-7BoxDraw a box around text at cursor position.

8CutinCut text into exisitng line (prompt provided)

9ConstructDraw a construction line

Esc cancels a pending operation.

Enter completes a pending operation.

All vectors are drawn in Pen 1, all text in Pen 2..

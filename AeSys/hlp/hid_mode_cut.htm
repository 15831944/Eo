Cut Mode to cut up a drawing

Select Dimension from MenuBar-Mode or

Press _ or <shift>PageUp from any GIN mode:

Press numeric keys to execute commands:

0 OptionsOption - Cut

1 TorchCursor cuts a single segment

2-2 SliceCuts segments intersecting the slice

3not used

4-4 FieldCuts segments at fence and Traps in

5not used

6not used

7-7 ClipClips a line into 3 segments

8not used

9 DivideDivides a line into specified # of segments

Cuts do not affect compressed segments. Segments must be expanded before they are cut. Polygons can be cut using 2-2 only.

Caution should be taken when using cut. Once a segment is cut, it remains fragmented and cannot be restored as it was before the cut procedure.

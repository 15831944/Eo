Trap Mode - Select Segments.

Choose Mode from MenuBar and click Trap or

Press - or PageUp from any GIN mode.

Press numeric keys to execute commands:

0 Switch IN/OUT Switch IN and OUT for the edit buffer.

1 Single Traps a single segment.

2-2 Stitch Stitch line and trap crossing.

4-4 Field Traps segments in field.

55... Last Traps segments in reverse order.

6 Engaged Traps engaged segment.

7 Compress Compress the Trap.

8 TrapMenu Operate on the Trap.

9 Modify Modifies trap segments.

A buffer is a subset of segments assembled from those in the Workfile. The buffer stays current across all operations, including other GIN modes.

MenuBar Edit displays the available Trap Commands.

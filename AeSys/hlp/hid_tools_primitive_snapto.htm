Q   Primitive Snap (Engage a primitive)

Move the cursor near the target primitive

      (but not beyond its endpoint) .

Press  Q to snap to and engage the nearest group. 

The primitive group blinks continuously to confirm engagement. 

The length and angle of an engaged Line primitives are reported.

Only 1 group may be engaged at a time.

If the cursor is  beyond the endpoint of the group, the group is ignored.

Pressing  Q  sequentially  snaps the cursor to the opposite end of the engaged primitive. 

Sequential operation of  Q  never moves the cursor off the engaged primitive.

Text segments are ignored.

Polygons are treated as a series of contiguous line segments.

Press SpaceBar to disengage the group.

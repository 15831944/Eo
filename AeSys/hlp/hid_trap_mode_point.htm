Trap 1 Traps segments coincident at a point.

To trap a single segment:

Position the cursor on the segment and press 1 .

This will place the single segment in the edit buffer.

To trap segments coincident at a point:

Position the cursor on the point and press 1 for each segment.

This will place all segments passing through the point in the edit buffer.

When Trap 0 - Remove has been selected, this operation removes the target segment from the buffer.

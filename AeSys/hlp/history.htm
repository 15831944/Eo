History:

We are consulting engineers in private practice; our firm has roots 70 years deep. We searched for a CAD system to speed production, to raise the quality of our documents, and reduce our drafting and design time.

Available CAD systems were too slow, too awkward, and too expensive, so we wrote Pegaesys.

It has been under development since 1980, and used exclusively since 1986 for more than a thousand construction projects.

The original version, written in Fortran, ran under VMS on Digital VAX computers, using Tektronix 42XX terminals. Since 1992, Pegaesys has been written in Microsoft Visual C++. This version runs under Windows, on personal computers. Potent features have been added. This is the next generation!

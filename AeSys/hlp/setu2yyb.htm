Constraints

A drawing may be constrained to fit tolerances of a grid or axis lock. This function provides a means to set these parameters and display their value.

InfluenceLines with less slope snap to Ortho axis.

Z-OffsetOffset angle of a rotatedOrtho axis.

Snap Set and enable the grid (in ruler inches).

Dot Display a 3-D grid of points.

Line Display a 3-D grid of lines.

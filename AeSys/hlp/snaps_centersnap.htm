B Center Snap (engaged segment )

First Engage a Segment

Then Press B to jump to the center point of the engaged segment.

If the segment is a line or arc, the move is to the midpoint.

If the segment is a circle or ellipse, the move is to the center point.

If the segment is a polygon, move is to center of engaged edge.

Pressing Q after a B always moves cursor left or down on the screen.

Pressing B after a T moves cursor to polygon centroid.

T Plane Snap (Engage a Polygon)

Move the cursor inside the panel.

Press T.

The cursor jumps to the surface of the polygon.

The polygon blinks to confirm engagement.

The area of an engaged polygon is reported.

Press SpaceBar to disengage the segment.

T Tangent Snap (Engage a circle or arc)

With a Draw 2 line pending:

Move the cursor near the arc.

Press T.

The cursor jumps to the tangent point of the pending line.

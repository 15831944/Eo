The Tutorial Drawings

You may use the Tutor drawings to learn Pegaesys if you wish. These are ordinary drawing files. You should make a back-up of these files before you totally obliterate them with your practice sessions.

Open a Tutor.JB1 drawing,

Overview the drawing, and

Zoom In on the topic of interest,.

Choose the MenuBar-File-Open command.

Select the file named Tutor1.

Open the file.

A drawing is displayed with topics arranged on the sheet like details.

Mouse the cursor to a topic and Press = Enter.

= is the shortcut key for MenuBar-View-Zoom

The square is a sweet spot target for the zoom.

Follow the instructions for each topic.

Press = and O to Overview Topics.

When you exit, do not save the Tutor drawing, choose MenuBar-Clear-All instead.
